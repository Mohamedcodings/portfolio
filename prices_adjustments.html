Product Price Adjustments

Optimizing Pricing Based on Demand, Expiry Dates, and Promotions

Skills Used

SQL, Python, Data Analysis, Business Strategy, Pricing Analysis

Problem Identification

Adjusting the prices of products based on demand, expiry dates, and promotional campaigns to maximize profit and efficiency.

Mission

To create a system that efficiently adjusts product prices based on demand, expiry dates, and promotional campaigns.

Challenges Faced

Managing large datasets and accurately adjusting prices based on complex factors like demand and expiry dates.

Outcome

The system helps businesses optimize their pricing strategy for different products, leading to increased profit and efficiency.
