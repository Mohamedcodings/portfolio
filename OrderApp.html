QuickPlat

Online Meal Ordering Platform

Skills Used

Java, Android Studio, XML

Problem Identification

Bridging the gap between customers and local restaurants by providing a user-friendly platform for meal ordering.

Mission

To provide an intuitive application that simplifies the process of ordering meals from local restaurants.

Challenges Faced

Creating a platform that is both intuitive and comprehensive, while also ensuring smooth integration with various restaurant menus.

Outcome

The platform redefines food ordering by offering a seamless experience for users to browse, favorite, and order meals efficiently.
