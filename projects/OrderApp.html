About the Project

Project Overview

As part of a university project, QuickPlat is designed to facilitate online meal ordering by connecting users with local restaurants through an intuitive interface. Inspired by market leaders such as UberEats, QuickPlat aims to enhance user experience with advanced search and order tracking features, ensuring transaction security. The project integrates TheMealDB API to present a variety of meals along with their descriptions, catering to diverse tastes and dietary preferences.

Problem Identification

The challenge was identifying a need for a more efficient, user-friendly, and secure platform to simplify online meal ordering between customers and restaurants.

Mission

To develop a cutting-edge online ordering system that improves the dining experience by facilitating easy discovery, ordering, and enjoyment of meals from a variety of local eateries.

Challenges Faced

Integrating a real-time menu and order system was a complex challenge, along with ensuring the app's performance.

Outcome

QuickPlat successfully launched as a school project, meeting all specified academic and functional requirements.
