Problem Identification

Hotel managers often spend significant time manually addressing client reviews, detracting from other critical tasks. The application automates this process, helping managers handle reviews more efficiently.

Mission

To develop an automated review management system that enables managers to respond to client feedback efficiently with tailored responses.

Challenges Faced

Ensuring the application's responses align with the hotel's brand voice while addressing client concerns accurately. Additionally, seamlessly integrating the system into existing review management workflows was challenging.

Outcome

The application drastically reduces the time required for review management by providing prompt and consistent responses, thus enhancing customer satisfaction. Managers can now focus on other critical aspects of hospitality while ensuring clients receive timely responses.
