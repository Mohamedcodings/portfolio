Daily Trip Forecasting with SARIMAX

Predicting Transport System Trip Demands

Skills Used

Pandas, Matplotlib, Statsmodels

Problem Identification

Predicting daily trips for the Ile-de-France transport system is challenging due to the variety of influencing factors and the volume of data.

Mission

To create a predictive tool that uses SARIMAX for anticipating future trip demands based on historical data.

Challenges Faced

Handling large datasets and accurately modeling trip trends and seasonality required specialized expertise.

Outcome

The tool provides a 30-day forecast, helping planners make informed decisions about future transport demand.
