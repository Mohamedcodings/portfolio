Hotel Query Response System

Efficient Customer Query Management

Inspiration and Journey

Recognizing the challenges hotel staff face in responding to numerous customer queries, this project aims to provide an automated and efficient solution using NLP. The goal was to create a system that could accurately understand and address customer questions quickly, allowing staff to focus on other tasks.

The system leverages advanced natural language processing techniques to understand and categorize customer queries, providing tailored responses that align with the hotel's brand tone and voice.

Skills Used

Natural Language Processing (NLP), Data Analysis, Python

Problem Identification

Hotels struggle with efficiently responding to a large volume of customer queries, which can impact customer satisfaction. This system helps automate the process, reducing the burden on staff and ensuring timely responses.

Mission

To create an NLP-based system that effectively manages customer queries, providing accurate and efficient responses to enhance customer satisfaction.

Challenges Faced

Ensuring the system's responses align with the hotel's brand voice while accurately addressing customer queries. It was challenging to train the model to understand the wide variety of questions typically asked by hotel guests.

Outcome

The system significantly improves response time for customer queries, enhancing overall customer satisfaction. Staff can now focus on providing better services without being overwhelmed by customer questions.
