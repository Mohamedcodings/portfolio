Marketplace Project

Building an Online Marketplace Platform

Skills Used

JavaScript, Python, SQL, Web Development, E-commerce

Problem Identification

Streamlining the buying and selling process to make it more accessible and efficient for users.

Mission

To create a marketplace platform that offers a seamless experience for buyers and sellers with advanced features.

Challenges Faced

Implementing features like group buying, bidding, and instant purchase options while maintaining a user-friendly interface.

Outcome

The platform connects buyers and sellers efficiently, enhancing the online shopping experience.
